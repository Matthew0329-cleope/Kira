About Kira Learning

Kira Learning is an innovative educational technology company dedicated to transforming K-12 education through the power of artificial intelligence. The company is focused on developing AI-native solutions that personalize learning, empower teachers, and improve educational outcomes.

Our Founders and Leadership

Kira Learning was co-founded and is led by a team of experienced entrepreneurs and technologists:

  • Andrea Pasinetti is a serial entrepreneur and software engineer. He is the co-founder and serves as the CEO of Kira Learning, driving the company's vision and strategy.
  • Jagriti Agrawal is the co-founder and VP of AI at Kira Learning. She leads the development of the company's cutting-edge AI technologies.
  • Andrew Ng serves as the Chairman of Kira Learning. A renowned figure in the field of AI, his expertise and guidance are invaluable to the company.

Our Mission

Kira Learning's mission is to revolutionize how students learn and how teachers teach by developing an AI-native learning platform. They strive to provide every learner with access to personalized, high-quality education and to equip educators with the tools they need to succeed.

Developing the AI-Powered Learning Platform

Kira Learning has developed a cutting-edge AI platform with a suite of tools designed to enhance the educational experience. Here's an overview of their approach:

  1. AI-Native Design: Unlike traditional tools that add AI as an afterthought, Kira integrates AI into every aspect of the learning process.
  2. Personalized Learning: Kira's AI Tutor provides individualized support to students, adapting to their learning style and pace.
  3. Empowering Teachers: The AI Teaching Assistant automates tasks like lesson planning and grading, freeing up teachers to focus on personalized student support.
  4. Data-Driven Insights: Kira provides administrators with real-time data on student engagement and learning outcomes, enabling informed decision-making.

Key Innovations

AI-Driven Personalization

Kira Learning leverages AI to create tailored learning experiences that cater to each student's unique needs.

Empowering Educators

Kira provides teachers with AI-powered tools that streamline their workflows and enhance their ability to support students.
